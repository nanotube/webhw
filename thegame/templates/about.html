Welcome to the Finance Game.

This site is an educational resource, designed to teach the student about various aspects of asset valuation in the setting of a competitive market based on asset auctions.

At this point, we are in a beta state, and you will see many changes in the near future.

In particular, documentation on auction mechanics, a friendly tutorial, and some cosmetic improvements are probably in order.
